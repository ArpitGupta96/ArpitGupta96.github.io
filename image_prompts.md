## Usage Tips:

1. **Copy & paste the entire prompt** into your AI image generator
2. **ChatGPT/DALL-E**: Paste directly into the chat
3. **Google Gemini**: Use "Generate images" feature
4. **Midjourney**: Prefix with `/imagine prompt:`
5. **For best results**: 
   - Use "High quality", "4K", "professional grade" modifiers
   - Specify style: "technical illustration", "3D render", "engineering diagram"
   - Include lighting: "studio lighting", "lab environment", "cinematic"

---

## Customization Tips:

- **Change colors**: Replace "cyan/blue" with "red/gold" or "purple/silver"
- **Change MCU**: Replace "STM32" with "NXP", "PIC", "ARM Cortex"
- **Add details**: Include specific component values like "100µF capacitors" or "IRF540N MOSFETs"
- **Adjust realism**: Use "photorealistic", "stylized", "technical drawing", or "CAD rendering"
